STEP 01

The Grid Resilience Challenge

Urban populations in sub-Saharan Africa are growing rapidly, and rising temperatures are pushing households toward mechanical cooling. But distribution transformers were sized for lighting and small appliance loads ‚Äî not AC. Without forward-looking load estimates tied to building stock characteristics, utilities cannot plan upgrades before the grid fails.
