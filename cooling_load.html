Cooling Load Impact

Forecasting electricity demand and grid strain in warming urban environments.

01 — Archetype Modeling

Mapped Microsoft building footprints to residential archetypes (materials and insulation) and used analytics to forecast cooling adoption and load profiles.

02 — Simulation Pipeline

SketchUp → EnergyPlus → JEPlus → Grid Analysis

Large-scale parametric simulations translate thermal loads into transformer-level strain estimates to guide grid-resilience planning under WHO comfort targets.
